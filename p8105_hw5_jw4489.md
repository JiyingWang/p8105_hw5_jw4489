##### Estimate the proportion of homicides that are unsolve.

``` r
baltimore_prop_test = 
  prop.test(df1_summarized |>
            filter(city_state == "Baltimore, MD") |>
            pull(unsolved_homicides),
            df1_summarized |>
            filter(city_state == "Baltimore, MD") |>
            pull(total_homicides))

baltimore_tidy = baltimore_prop_test |>
  broom::tidy()

baltimore_tidy
```
